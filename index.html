About

This idea first started as just a way to cope with my anxiety, depression and bipolar disorders. Most of my life I had coped with my symptoms and was high functioning. In February of 2018 I completely broke down, and I could not work, cook, clean or take care of myself. I was sent to a group and it helped me so much. Now, I am not cured or never have symptoms. This is something I have to maintain for the rest of my life. My painting is a way for me to be passionate and take pride in something.

I hope you enjoy my art as much as I do. Thank you for looking at my art and my site! 

Love, Anna
